Dear Hiring Team,

Acceleration Partners is not hiring this role merely to add AI features. The harder mandate is to turn proprietary data, recurring client work, and emerging AI capability into a governed product portfolio that account teams trust, clients experience as better service, and the business can scale without commoditizing expert judgment.

That is the operating problem I have spent my career learning to solve from several angles. At Vape-Jet, I work directly across a software-enabled robotics business to connect production, quality, customer, support, ERP, telemetry, and engineering signals into AI-assisted workflows, dashboards, standard work, and accountability. Through DudeWorth, I design AI opportunity and agentic workflow patterns around value, feasibility, structured context, retrieval, human authority, evaluation, escalation, governance, and adoption. At Amazon, I built mechanisms for a distributed, 24/7 customer-backward operation. At Compunetics, I led engineering, manufacturing, quality, and delivery work where technical innovation had to survive high-consequence controls and cross-functional handoffs.

I have not held the formal title of AI Product Director inside a partnership marketing agency, and I would not blur that distinction. My transfer case is direct operating evidence: I know how to enter an unfamiliar workflow with humility, surface the decisions and constraints, connect technical and business owners, establish the human contract, build a bounded mechanism, instrument it, and make the learning reusable.

My independent candidate vision at https://russelldudek.github.io/acceleration/ frames the role through a Service-to-Product Fold: a decision model for determining what should remain human-led, what should assist, what can become agentic, and what earns full productization. The accompanying 120-day plan shows how I would learn AP's actual workflows before prescribing a roadmap, then prove, package, and compound only what creates observable client and operating value.

I would welcome the chance to test this thinking against a real AP workflow and a difficult product decision. That conversation would reveal more than another list of AI tools ever could.

Carpe diem,
